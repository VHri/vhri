About Me

I am currently pursuing a Masters of Science in Computer Science at Boston University - Graduate School of Arts and Sciences. I am actively seeking summer internships and full-time positions in software development, specializing in full-stack development, as well as data engineering and analytics.

My academic journey commenced at Jaypee Institute of Information Technology, Noida, where I earned a Bachelors in Technology in Computer Science and Engineering. During my undergraduate years, I cultivated a robust foundation in programming and discovered my fervor for data science. Proficient in Python, SQL, R, and Tableau, I not only excelled in machine learning but also applied my skills to real-world scenarios, mastering both supervised and unsupervised algorithms.

Stepping into the professional realm, I contributed to the success of Paytm as a DevOps Engineer in the Central Data Platform (CDP) team. Leading the design and development of a web portal for internal tools, I implemented an automation module linked to internal LDAP, significantly reducing onboarding times and manual efforts. My initiatives included streamlining user onboarding processes for internal querying interfaces (Apache Hive) using Jenkins jobs, saving valuable time and enhancing efficiency. Additionally, I created an alerting system for AWS Service Cost Deviations, which has become instrumental for the Risk and Data Science teams.

My commitment to making data-driven decisions, coupled with my diverse skill set, positions me as a valuable asset capable of transforming conceptual ideas into impactful solutions. I am excited about the opportunity to contribute to innovative projects and drive success in a dynamic software development environment.
